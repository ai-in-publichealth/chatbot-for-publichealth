LLM-driven Chatbot for Public Health Information

A Research Proposal

SY Siu

Introduction

  • Large Language Models (LLMs) revolutionizing NLP
  • Potential in improving health information access
  • Challenges in applying LLMs to public health

Literature Review Highlights

  • LLM chatbots improve user engagement (Xu et al., 2023)
  • Domain-specific fine-tuning crucial (Chen et al., 2024)
  • Hallucination risks in LLMs (Maynez et al., 2020)
  • RAG techniques reduce inaccuracies (Wang et al., 2024)

Research Purpose

Compare approaches for developing accurate public health chatbots

  • Explore fine-tuning methods
  • Implement RAG and CoT techniques
  • Develop specialized datasets
  • Comprehensive performance evaluation

Methodology

  • Base Models: Qwen, Yi
  • Fine-tuning: QLora, prefix tuning, prompt tuning
  • Advanced Techniques: RAG, CoT
  • Custom Datasets
  • Evaluation: MMLU, BLEU, ROUGE, Human evaluation
